Who we are

About Timothy Lutheran

A diverse city church in St. Louis — rooted in grace, sent to the world.

Our vision

Planted Here.
Sent Everywhere.

Planted in culturally diverse St. Louis. Sent to the world as a congregation alive to the Gospel that breaks every wall — welcoming every person, bearing bold witness to the saving grace of God through Jesus, from our neighborhood to the nations.

Our mission

Gathered Around Grace. Sent in Love.

Timothy Lutheran Church is a congregation alive to the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Gathered in worship around the Word and Sacraments of the risen Lord, we accept every person as one for whom Christ died. Growing in faith through Christian education and honest community, we go — from our neighborhood to the nations — bearing the tangible love of God to all.

We are a congregation of the Lutheran Church — Missouri Synod (LCMS), rooted in the Lindenwood Park neighborhood of St. Louis.

Worship

Gathering as God's people, celebrating His grace, receiving His gifts of Word and Sacrament.

Acceptance

Intentionally welcoming and loving all people as Jesus does.

Sharing the love of Jesus with those who don't yet know Him.

Worship at Timothy

Where Tradition Meets the Unexpected

At Timothy, we believe that the beauty of holiness isn't confined to a single era or instrument. We are a community deeply rooted in the Lutheran liturgical tradition, yet we remain vibrantly open to the full spectrum of musical gifts God has placed in our hands.

If you are looking for a worship experience that feels both grounded and adventurous, you've found your home.

Music & Sound

A Soundscape as Wide as Grace

We take worship seriously, but we don't believe "traditional" has to mean "predictable." On any given Sunday, our sanctuary becomes a meeting place for the ancient and the eclectic.

You might hear:

  • The thunderous resonance of a pipe organ.
  • The delicate shimmer of a hammered dulcimer or handbell choir.
  • The rhythmic pulse of a drum kit or steel drums.
  • The soulful soaring of a violin, trumpet, or an African choir.

Sometimes, you'll hear them all in the same service. We don't mix these styles for the sake of novelty; we do it because the grace we celebrate is just as wide-ranging and diverse as the music we make.

Rooted & Fresh

Deeply Rooted. Spiritually Fresh.

Our worship follows the timeless patterns of the liturgy, providing a "unique — but familiar" rhythm to your week. We value the substance of our heritage while making room for the Holy Spirit to surprise us.

  • Weekly Communion: We celebrate the Eucharist every Sunday, centering our lives on the tangible promise of God's presence.
  • Theologically Rich: Our music is chosen to reflect the depth of the Gospel, ensuring that what we sing and play nourishes the soul.
  • A Place for Every Voice: Whether you are a master of a rare instrument or simply love to sing from the pew, there is a place for your "joyful noise" here.

Why we give

Participation, not obligation.

Giving at Timothy is not an obligation — it is a participation with God in what he is already doing in the world. We give because God has given. Every gift supports the life of this congregation: Sunday worship, Word of Life School, our food pantry, youth ministry, and the neighbors God places in our path each week.

You are always welcome to come and simply receive that Sunday's grace.

Online giving

Give securely online

Our online giving is processed by Tithe.ly, a trusted platform used by over 37,000 churches. Give by credit card, debit card, or ACH bank transfer — one-time or recurring. No account required. A tax receipt is emailed automatically after every gift.

You will be taken to Tithe.ly's secure giving page.

Stephen Ministry

You Don't Have to Face This Alone

Trained lay caregivers walking alongside people in life's hardest seasons.

What is Stephen Ministry?

One-to-one Christian care

Stephen Ministry equips congregation members to provide confidential, one-to-one Christian care to people going through difficult times — grief, illness, job loss, divorce, loneliness, or any of life's challenges. A trained Stephen Minister walks alongside you as a caring presence for as long as you need.

Stephen Ministers are not counselors or therapists. They are listeners — people who have been trained to be fully present, to keep confidence, and to bring the compassion of Christ to someone who is hurting.

Request a Caregiver

If you or someone you know is going through a difficult time, reaching out is the first step. Everything is kept strictly confidential.

About the market

A neighborhood tradition

The Timothy Christmas Market is an annual outdoor market bringing the Lindenwood Park community together each December. Local artisans and vendors set up in the church grounds — offering handmade goods, food, and festive fun for families and neighbors of all kinds.

The market is free and open to the public. It's one of our favorite ways to be a blessing to the neighborhood God has planted us in.
